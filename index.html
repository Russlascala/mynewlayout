About

Speciality Associates located in Anytown, USA is dedicated to providing exceptional, personalized health care for our patients. Our practice specializes in diseases that affect our patients. We focus on the prevention of these illnesses through wellness care, as well as treating chronic disorders that may arise. By creating an ongoing personal relationship with our patients, our practice is able to provide you with a health oriented means of communication throughout your lifetime. Utilizing the latest advances in medicine combined with the state-of-the-art techniques, our goal is to treat our patients with comfort and compassion.

Office Policies

Appointments & Cancellations

You may schedule an appointment with a patient care coordinator.

With the exception of serious emergencies, it is expected that you keep your appointments. If you need to reschedule an appointment, it is necessary that you advise the office at least 24 hours in advance. Your consideration in this matter will allow me to accommodate other patients who could benefit from an appointment.
